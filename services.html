Interpreting Services offered at Combridge

We are a group qualified of interpreters in different parts of Sweden. We want to bring efficiency and timely interpreting contracts to both private and public sectors. We want to provide quality interpreting services with less costs.


Face-to-face

In our face-to-face interpreting a professional interpreter comes physically to you and your client. There is always extra advantage with direct interpreting.

Video

In the case where the customer wants to minimise the cost and is physically far away from our office, we leverage the technology and make a quality interpreting through video link.

Telephone

In the absence of both video and physical contact there is still the possibility of contacting via telephone. Combridge avails the telephone contact of the interprter to the customer.
